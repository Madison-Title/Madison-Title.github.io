Madison Title & Escrow, LLC was created by a talented title veteran who felt she could offer her clients more personalized, nimble and affordable options for their real estate closing and title insurance needs. We provide a full range of real estate legal and transactional services including title insurance, escrow and closing services to homeowners, buyers, realtors, relocation companies and asset management companies.

Madison Title services the legal and lending communities as well as developers, government agencies, municipalities and homeowners. Our philosophy is simple. We are in a service industry and service is our number one priority. Our goal is to provide you with fast, accurate information that enables you, or your client, to make sound business decisions. We are dedicated to our consistent and competitive turnaround time, continually striving to meet your deadlines.

From the time we receive your initial order thru delivery of the final policy, Madison Title & Escrow performs and reviews every facet in-house, which means you can rest easy knowing that we will meet your timelines, whatever they may be. We are a team of listeners who will work to solve problems before they delay or cancel transactions. Madison Title is focused on you, our customer.

Madison Title is underwritten by Fidelity National Title Insurance Company and has an office on West End Avenue in Nashville to service you. We invite you to get to know our staff and would be honored to service any of your closing, escrow or title insurance needs.
